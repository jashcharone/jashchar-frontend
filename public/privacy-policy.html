1. Introduction

Welcome to Jashchar ERP ("we," "our," or "us"). Jashchar ERP is a comprehensive School and College Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) platform developed by Jashchar Technologies. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web platform.

Our Commitment: We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of all personal data entrusted to us by educational institutions, students, parents, and staff members.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Student Information: Name, date of birth, gender, admission number, class, section, blood group, address, parent/guardian details, emergency contacts, and academic records
  • Staff Information: Name, employee ID, designation, department, contact details, qualification, experience, and employment records
  • Parent/Guardian Information: Name, relationship to student, contact number, email address, occupation, and communication preferences
  • Account Information: Email address, username, password (encrypted), role, and permissions

2.2 Educational Data

  • Attendance records and biometric data (if applicable)
  • Examination marks, grades, and academic performance
  • Fee payment history and financial transactions
  • Library records and resource usage
  • Transport and hostel allocation details
  • Timetables and class schedules

2.3 Technical Information

  • Device information (type, operating system, unique identifiers)
  • IP address and location data (with consent)
  • App usage analytics and crash reports
  • Login timestamps and session information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

Purpose Description
Educational Management Managing student admissions, attendance, examinations, and academic records
Communication Sending notifications about fees, events, attendance, and academic updates
Financial Processing Processing fee payments, generating receipts, and financial reporting
Administrative Functions HR management, payroll, transport, hostel, and library operations
Security & Compliance Ensuring data security, preventing fraud, and regulatory compliance
Service Improvement Analyzing usage patterns to improve our services

4. Data Storage and Security

Multi-Tenant Architecture: Jashchar ERP uses a secure multi-tenant architecture where each organization's data is logically isolated using organization_id, branch_id, and session_id parameters, ensuring complete data separation between institutions.

4.1 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission (TLS/SSL)
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive data (passwords, financial information)
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) with granular permissions
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies at database level
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Automatic session timeout and secure authentication
  • Multi-factor authentication support

4.2 Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, and support educational record-keeping requirements. Academic records may be retained indefinitely as per educational regulations.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With Educational Institution: Data is accessible to authorized staff of your registered institution
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (Supabase), payment processors, and SMS/notification services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government authority
  • With Consent: When explicit consent is provided by the data subject or institution

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for optional data processing
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with relevant data protection authorities

7. Children's Privacy

Jashchar ERP processes data of students including minors (children under 18 years). This data is collected and processed with the consent of educational institutions and parents/guardians. We implement additional safeguards for children's data:

  • Parental consent required for student registration
  • Limited data collection to educational necessities
  • No direct marketing to children
  • Restricted data sharing for minors' information

8. Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database hosting and authentication
  • Payment Gateways: For fee collection (Razorpay/other providers)
  • SMS Services: For sending notifications
  • Cloud Storage: For document and media storage

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing data handling.

9. Cookies and Tracking

Our web platform uses essential cookies for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • User preferences and settings
  • Security and fraud prevention

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

10. International Data Transfer

Your data may be stored and processed on servers located in India and other countries through our cloud service providers. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified through the app or email. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000 (India)
  • Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011
  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for applicable users
